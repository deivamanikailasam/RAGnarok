# Refund Policy

This document describes the refund policy for Helios subscriptions. It is the official,
current policy and supersedes all earlier drafts.

## Overview

Customers may request a refund within a tier-dependent window measured from the invoice date.
Refunds are processed to the original payment method within 10 business days.

## Refund Windows by Tier

| Tier | Billing | Refund Window | Notes |
|------|---------|---------------|-------|
| Consumer | Monthly | 14 days | Self-service via the billing portal |
| Pro | Monthly | 14 days | Self-service via the billing portal |
| Pro | Annual | 30 days | Contact support to initiate |
| Enterprise | Annual | 30 days | Governed by the signed order form |

## Enterprise Exceptions

Enterprise customers on a signed annual contract have a 30-day refund window from the invoice
date. Custom terms in the order form override this default. Mid-term downgrades are not refundable
but are credited to the next renewal.

## How to Request

Consumer and Pro monthly customers self-serve in the billing portal. Enterprise and annual
customers must contact support, who verify the contract terms before issuing a refund.
